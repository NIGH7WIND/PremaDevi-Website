Our Mission

At Prema Devi Foundation, we are dedicated to creating positive change in our communities. Our mission is to empower underprivileged children through education and healthcare, while also supporting farmers to achieve sustainable livelihoods. We believe in the power of collective action to transform lives and build a brighter future for all.

Underprivileged Children Health and Education

Our mission is to provide quality healthcare and education to underprivileged children, ensuring they have the opportunity to thrive and reach their full potential. Through our initiatives, we aim to break the cycle of poverty and create a brighter future for generations to come.

Farmers and FPOs Aid

We support farmers and Farmer Producer Organizations (FPOs) by leveraging technology (Data / AI /ML models), providing essential inputs such as seeds and fertilizers as well as necessary supply chain for delivery of farmer's produce to the customers at better prices. Our goal is to empower rural communities, enhance agricultural productivity, and promote sustainable farming practices that benefit both farmers and the environment.
